# TRIP HQ — Updated Product Brief
### Full CRUD + Media Management Layer · Internal Travel Operations Portal

---

## What Changed

The previous brief covered the pages. This update covers the **data layer** — everything that gets captured, uploaded, stored, and managed throughout the trip. This portal isn't just a viewer. It's a working ops tool that gets filled in live, across 10 days, across 6 cities, often on a phone.

Every single piece of information captured on this trip — a business card photo, a voice note from a supplier meeting, a product photo from Yiwu market floor, a train ticket PDF, an expense receipt — needs a home here. Not in WhatsApp. Not in Google Photos. Here.

---

## Core Principle

**Every record in this portal must support: Create, Read, Update, Delete, and Upload.**

No read-only pages. If data can be captured on the trip, it must be addable from the portal. If a mistake is made, it must be editable. If something is irrelevant, it must be deletable. Simple.

---

## What Needs CRUD — Page by Page

---

### Itinerary
**Create:** Add a new day entry or add a sub-note to an existing day mid-trip when plans change

**Update:** Edit any field inline — morning plan, evening plan, hotel name, meeting time. Plans change on the ground. The Foshan meeting might move to a different time. That should take 10 seconds to update.

**Delete:** Remove a day note or sub-entry that's no longer relevant

**Uploads needed:**
- Attach a voice note per day (recorded on phone, uploaded here) — end-of-day debrief captured as audio
- Attach any document to a day — e.g., the Foshan furniture mall printed catalogue, a restaurant receipt as a memory marker
- Attach photos per day for reference

---

### Contacts
**Create:** Add a new contact on the ground — someone met unexpectedly at the Yiwu market, a referral from Allen, a supplier who walked up at Huaqiangbei

**Update:** Fill in WeChat ID after exchanging, update meeting status from Upcoming → Met → Followed Up, add notes after the meeting, mark card collected

**Delete:** Remove a contact that turned out to be irrelevant

**Uploads needed:**
- **Business card photo** — the most critical upload of this entire portal. Take a photo of the card, attach to the contact. Both front and back. This replaces CamCard.
- Profile photo of the person (optional, from LinkedIn or in-person)
- Any document they shared — catalogue PDF, price list, product spec sheet
- Voice note attached to a contact — recorded right after leaving the meeting while impressions are fresh. "Allen was great, hero SKUs are X, Y, Z, pricing was better than expected…"

---

### Catalogue Builder
**Create:** Add a product on the market floor. Fast form — category, name, CNY price, MOQ, franchise fit score, notes. Should take under 60 seconds per product.

**Update:** Edit price after negotiating, update franchise fit after reflection, change status from Shortlisted → Confirmed → Rejected, add sub-category, fix a typo in product name

**Delete:** Remove a product that got rejected

**Uploads needed:**
- **Product photo** — photograph the product right in the stall, upload from phone. This is core. A catalogue without photos is useless for franchise stores.
- Multiple photos per product (front, back, packaging, price tag)
- Supplier's own catalogue page — if they hand you a printed spec sheet, photograph it and attach to the product
- Price list document from a supplier — PDF or photo

---

### Budget Tracker
**Create:** Log an expense immediately after paying. Date, city, category, amount, currency, description.

**Update:** Fix the amount if entered wrong, correct the category, change the currency

**Delete:** Remove a duplicate entry

**Uploads needed:**
- **Receipt photo** — photograph the bill or receipt, attach to the expense entry
- Hotel invoice PDF
- Transaction screenshot from Alipay or WeChat Pay as proof

---

### Pre-Departure Checklist
**Create:** Add a new task that wasn't in the original list — something that came up

**Update:** Mark complete, add a deadline, add notes like "VPN tested on Mar 10 — working"

**Delete:** Remove tasks that are not applicable

**Uploads needed:**
- TDAC registration confirmation screenshot
- Visa confirmation scan / photo
- Travel insurance policy PDF
- Hotel booking confirmations PDF
- Any ticket or booking reference as PDF/image

---

### Packing List
**Create:** Add an item that wasn't in the original list — something you remembered

**Update:** Mark as packed, update quantity, add a note

**Delete:** Remove something you decided not to bring

**No uploads needed on this page.** It's a checklist, keep it fast.

---

### Transport
**Create:** Add a leg that wasn't planned — an unplanned DiDi, a bus, a local ferry in Bangkok

**Update:** Add booking reference after booking, update status to Booked, correct timing

**Delete:** Remove a leg that's no longer happening

**Uploads needed:**
- **Train / flight ticket PDF or screenshot** — upload the actual ticket for each leg. Boarding pass, booking confirmation, everything in one place. Never searching through emails for a ticket at the station.
- QR code screenshot of train booking (critical for China G-trains — they need the QR at the gate)

---

### Content Planner
**Create:** Add a new shot idea on the ground — something you see and want to film

**Update:** Change status from Planned → Shot, add notes about what was actually filmed vs planned, log the equipment used

**Delete:** Remove a shot that didn't work out

**Uploads needed:**
- **Thumbnail / reference photo** — a quick photo of what the shot looks like or a reference for editing
- Raw video file upload (if small enough) or just a Google Drive / iCloud link field
- Edited video link (YouTube unlisted, Google Drive, etc.)

---

### Post-Trip Deliverables
**Create:** Add a new deliverable that emerged from the trip — something agreed upon in a meeting that wasn't anticipated

**Update:** Move status from Pending → In Progress → Done, add completion notes, link to the delivered asset

**Delete:** Remove a deliverable that's no longer needed

**Uploads needed:**
- Final catalogue file (the completed Google Sheet exported as PDF or Excel)
- Agreement documents signed with Charlie or others
- Any reports or summaries generated after the trip

## Global File & Document Management

Beyond page-specific uploads, there needs to be a **Documents Hub** — one place where every file uploaded across the entire trip lives, searchable and filterable.

Think of it as the filing cabinet for the trip.

**What gets stored here:**
- All business card scans (tagged by contact name)
- All ticket PDFs and booking confirmations
- All expense receipts
- All supplier catalogues and price lists received
- All product photos from the catalogue build
- All voice notes from meetings and end-of-day debriefs
- Signed or draft agreements
- Any photos taken at markets for reference

**How it needs to work:**
- Upload from phone camera directly (mobile-optimized upload button)
- Tag every file: Document Type + City + Contact (optional) + Day number (optional)
- Search by tag, city, contact name, or document type
- Preview in browser — photos inline, PDFs in viewer, audio playable
- Download any file
- Delete any file
- Bulk download by tag (e.g., "Download all Yiwu product photos")

**File types to support:** JPG, PNG, PDF, MP3, M4A, MP4, WebM, HEIC (iPhone photos)

**Storage:** Supabase Storage buckets — one bucket per category or one bucket with folder structure by city/day

## External App Links Manager

A simple page or section within the sidebar that stores all the external tools used during the trip. Not bookmarks — a managed list with context.

**What gets stored:**
- App name
- URL or deep link
- Category (Transport / Payment / Communication / Navigation / Business)
- Notes field ("Use this to book G-trains — log in with +91 number")
- Quick-launch button

**Examples of what goes here:**
- Trip.com — G-train bookings
- DiDi app — China ride-hailing
- Alipay — payments
- WeChat — contacts and payments
- Amap — China maps
- Airalo — eSIM top-up
- Supabase dashboard — if something needs to be checked directly
- Google Drive folder link — shared trip documents
- Faire / EazyToSell dashboard links

This saves the mental overhead of remembering which app to open for what. One place, every tool, labelled clearly.

## Voice Notes — Specific Requirement

Voice notes need first-class support because the most valuable capture during a sourcing trip happens verbally — impressions right after a meeting, product thoughts while walking the market floor, end-of-day summaries while tired.

**Where voice notes attach:**
- To a contact (post-meeting debrief)
- To a catalogue product (why this product was shortlisted or rejected)
- To a day in the itinerary (end-of-day summary)
- Standalone in Documents Hub (general capture)

**How it should work:**
- Upload an M4A or MP3 file (recorded on iPhone Voice Memos or any recorder)
- Playable inline in the browser without downloading
- Labeled with duration, upload date, and attached entity (contact name or day)
- Optional: text notes field alongside the audio for a quick summary

No live recording in the browser needed — just clean upload and playback.

---

## Mobile Experience — Non-Negotiable

The Budget Tracker, Packing List, Contacts (card collection), Catalogue (product photos), and Documents Hub will all be used on a phone while physically in markets, restaurants, and hotel rooms.

These pages specifically must:
- Have large tap targets (nothing small to press with a thumb)
- Camera upload that opens the phone camera directly — not a file picker that requires navigating folders
- Fast forms — minimum required fields to create a record, optional fields collapsible
- Work on 4G / spotty China network — forms should save optimistically and sync when connection recovers
- No heavy animations that slow down older phones

## What Good Looks Like

Standing in a Yiwu market stall, phone in hand:
1. Open catalogue page
2. Hit "Add Product"
3. Tap the camera icon, photograph the product
4. Enter name, CNY price, MOQ, hit franchise fit 4/5
5. Save

That should take 45 seconds. If it takes longer, the form is too complex.

Same for contacts. Walk out of a meeting with Allen:
1. Open contacts page
2. Tap Allen's card
3. Hit "Mark as Met"
4. Photograph his business card front and back — attached instantly
5. Hit record voice note — upload the M4A from Voice Memos
6. Save

That should take 2 minutes. If it takes longer, the UX is wrong.

That's the bar.
